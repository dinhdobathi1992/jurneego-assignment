How JurneeGo works

Shared Learning, by design

  • •Child asks a question in a moderated AI chat
  • •AI responds safely and age-appropriately
  • •Parents and teachers see the learning journey
  • •Presence is active — no silent monitoring
  • •Adults guide through annotations, not overrides

Why SSA matters

SSA replaces black-box AI with transparency and trust. Children keep agency. Adults stay meaningfully involved. Learning stays collaborative.

Learning by Design, Not Just Answers

JurneeGo supports long-term learning through Understanding by Design (UbD), helping parents and teachers plan backward from meaningful goals.

UbD Element Example
Long-Term Goal Develop curiosity and foundational understanding of how technology shapes the world.
Enduring Understanding Technology is designed by people and can be understood, questioned, and improved.
Essential Questions How do computers "think"? Why do we design rules for technology?
Daily AI Interactions Guided conversations with AI that build on these questions over time.
Adult Guidance Parent and teacher annotations that connect curiosity to bigger ideas.
