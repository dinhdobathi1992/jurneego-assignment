How JurneeGo works

Built on a Shared Session Architecture (SSA). Patent filed.

Shared learning, by design

Most AI tools are built for one person. JurneeGo is built for shared learning. A child's AI conversation can be mirrored to parents and teachers in real time.

  • 1 Child asks a question in a moderated AI chat
  • 2 AI responds safely and age-appropriately
  • 3 Parents see the conversation in real-time
  • 4 Presence is active — no silent monitoring
  • 5 Adults guide through annotations, not overrides

Why SSA matters

SSA replaces black-box AI with transparency and trust. Children keep agency. Adults stay meaningfully involved. Learning stays collaborative.

Designed with consent, auditability, and alignment with leading child protection frameworks from day one.
